About

So you have to log your blood glucose and insulin. What a drag. Well, it doesn't have to be.

See, thats the thing with Test Strip, it conforms to your life. Not only can you graph your insulin and blood glucose, you can share your data with your doctor.

Features

  • Advanced Graphing
  • Apple Watch Companion
  • HealthKit Sync
  • Data Sharing
